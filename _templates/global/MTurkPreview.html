DO NOT ACCEPT THIS HIT UNLESS you previously completed the screening survey, and were specifically invited to participate. Thank you.

This HIT is an academic not-for-profit research study on decision making. It will require you to interact with other workers, and make decisions, in real time.

While this research is voluntary, in order to protect the integrity of the study, please start the experiment immediately after reading these instructions, stay throughout the approximately 40 minutes of experiment, and click through the 100 rounds as quickly as possible.

You will be paid $2.00 for showing up, up to an additional $4.00 based on your performance, and an additional $2.00 for completion. If another participant abandons or returns the HIT before the experiment is completed, you will receive $2.00 for showing up plus payment for the rounds completed.

In order to be paid you must start this HIT within 3 minutes of accepting it. If you return or abandon the HIT before the end of the experiment, you will not be paid, and the other participants will not receive the full payment possible for completion.

Thank you.

Participation in this HIT is voluntary. You may discontinue your participation at any time without penalty. If you choose to discontinue participation, you will not receive payment for the HIT.

Additionally, this research is for residents of the United States over the age of 18; if you are not a resident of the United States and/or under the age of 18, please do not accept the HIT.

Privacy:

  1. The data you provide may be collected and used by Amazon as per its privacy agreement. However, none of your personal information will be gathered or stored by this study. Although every reasonable effort has been taken, confidentiality during actual internet communication procedures cannot be guaranteed.
  2. Data may exist on backups or server logs beyond the timeframe of this research project.
